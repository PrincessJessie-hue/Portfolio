From Figma to Cloud: The Self-Taught Journey of a Perfectionist.

Aloha, I'm Ayanda. I'm a self-taught UI/UX designer and web developer with a passion for building digital spaces that feel as good as they look. While my aesthetic is all about soft palettes and "princess" vibes, my work ethic is anything but soft.

The Journey

I taught myself how to code and design from the ground up. I'm a natural problem-solver, but I'll be honest, my biggest weakness is that I don't know how to quit. If there's a bug in the code or a pixel out of place, I won't stop until it's perfect. I don't just aim to finish; I aim to succeed.

The Tech Side

I am deeply in love with everything tech. Currently, I'm diving into the world of Cloud Computing and studying for my Microsoft Azure AZ-900 certification. Whether it's crafting a responsive layout in CSS or architecting solutions in Azure, I'm always leveling up.

Off the Clock

When I'm not staring at a terminal or Figma canvas, you can find me:

  • Practicing Pilates to clear my mind.
  • Enjoying the outdoors and soaking up fresh inspiration.
  • Obsessing over interior design (because a beautiful room is just physical UI).
  • Sipping tea while dreaming up my next big project.
