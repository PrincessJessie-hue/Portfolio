Project Overview

Calmora is a mindfulness and relaxation app designed to help users manage stress through guided meditation, breathing exercises, and mood tracking. Many people struggle with stress but find existing meditation apps overwhelming or difficult to use. This project explores a simple and user-friendly solution to encourage mindfulness in daily life.

Problem Statement

Many users struggle to maintain daily wellness habits due to busy schedules and lack of guidance.

Goals

Design an intuitive mobile app that encourages consistent wellness routines and promotes mental well-being.
